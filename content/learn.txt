PEDIATRIC CANCER BASICS

A pediatric cancer diagnosis can feel overwhelming.
It is normal to feel confused, scared, or unsure where to start.

WHAT CAREGIVERS SHOULD KNOW
• You are not expected to remember everything
• Questions are always appropriate
• Your care team wants you to ask for clarification

WHEN TO CALL THE CARE TEAM
• Fever
• Changes in behavior
• Concerns that worry you
